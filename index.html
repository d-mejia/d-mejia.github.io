Biography:

Hello! My name is Daniela. I am currently a Research Assistant at the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center, working for Jullie W. Pan, M.D., Ph.D. and Hoby Hetherington, Ph.D. in Neurology and Radiology. I coordinate and assist with 3T and 7T neuroimaging studies on veterans with mTBI and/or PTSD, epilepsy patients, neuro-oncology patients, and healthy controls. These studies use an imaging technique known as magnetic resonance spectroscopy to study the metabolic processes in the brain. Outside of work, I also take night classes at the University of Pittsburgh in math and computer science.


In the fall of 2016, I plan on applying to graduate programs with a focus on neuroimaging analysis and/or Quantitative Psychology.
