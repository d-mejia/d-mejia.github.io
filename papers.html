Hello! My name is Daniela. I am currently a second-year graduate student in Quantitative Psychology at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I work with Drs. Gabriele Gratton and Monica Fabiani in the Cognitive Neuroimaging Lab, and with Dr. Xiaofeng Shao from the Statistics department. My main research area is in time-frequency analysis of EEG data.
